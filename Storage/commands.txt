List of Commands:

-help - Returns this List of Commands
-ping - Pings the bot
-balance - returns your balance
-balance @[user] - returns specified user's balance
-beg - if you're lucky, you'll be given a small amount of money
-rob @[user] - if you're lucky, you'll manage to steal a small amount of money
-give [amount] @[user] - gives the specified amount to the specified user
